I am a PhD candidate in Epidemiology and Population Health at Stanford University School of Medicine, advised by Dr. David Rehkopf. My training bridges clinical medicine (MBBS) and population health (MPH, Harvard T.H. Chan). My work asks when an observed health disparity reflects something causal — and what to do about it when it does. Before Stanford, I trained at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai University of Traditional Chinese Medicine, and the Harvard T.H.
